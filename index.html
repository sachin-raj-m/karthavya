About The Event

About The Event

Join the Movement for a Greener Tomorrow!

Are you ready to be a changemaker and lead the way in revolutionizing solid waste management? This is your chance to make a real impact on the world's environmental challenges. Be part of our innovative hackathon and contribute to the vision of making Kerala the cleanest state in the country!

Event Highlights

Innovate for Impact

  • Address real-world challenges in waste generation, disposal, recycling, and sustainability.
  • Contribute to the "Litter Free Kerala" Campaign under Navakeralam Karma Padhathi.

Collaborate with Experts

  • Engage with professionals from various fields, and the Kerala Institute of Local Administration (KILA).
  • Network with engineers, designers, and environmental scientists.

Make a Difference

  • Your ideas matter! Help shape the future of waste management and contribute to a cleaner, healthier planet.
  • Work on practical solutions that align with the goals of the State government.

Why Participate?

Diverse Talents Welcome

  • Whether you're a coder, designer, engineer, scientist, or a concerned citizen, your unique perspective is vital in finding sustainable solutions.

Recognition and Prizes

  • Stand a chance to win exciting prizes and gain recognition for your innovative contributions.
  • Showcase your skills and ideas to a wider audience.

Global Impact

  • Be part of a global movement addressing one of the most critical environmental issues.
  • Contribute to a cleaner and more sustainable future for Kerala and beyond.

Addressing the Challenge

SAFE AND SCIENTIFIC SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT

Addressing the surge in waste generation requires innovative, sustainable solutions for safe disposal, recycling, and management. Our hackathon theme encourages participants to explore cutting-edge technologies, community initiatives, and policy interventions. By fostering a collective effort for practical, science-based solutions, we aim to contribute to a cleaner, healthier planet. Join us in envisioning a future where solid waste is managed with safety, efficiency, and environmental consciousness, paving the way for sustainability.

Join the Movement

How to Participate?

  1. Register: Secure your spot by registering for the hackathon.
  2. Form Teams: Collaborate with like-minded individuals or join a team during the event.
  3. Innovate: Dive into challenges, brainstorm ideas, and develop practical solutions.
  4. Present: Showcase your solutions to a panel of judges and stand a chance to win!

Get Involved!

Ready to be part of the solution? Join us in driving positive change and creating a lasting impact on solid waste management. Together, let's make Kerala the cleanest state and contribute to a sustainable future for generations to come.
